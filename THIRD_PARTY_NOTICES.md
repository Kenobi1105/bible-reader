# Third-Party Notices

Scripture Desk fetches the following Bible text and linguistic resources on demand. Except where noted below, no Bible text is bundled with this repository. This notice is part of the application distribution and should be retained with public releases.

### Westminster Leningrad Codex (WLC)

The WLC is obtained through GetBible/CrossWire. CrossWire lists the [WLC distribution licence as Public Domain](https://www.crosswire.org/sword/copyright/ModInfoCopyright.jsp?modName=WLC).

### Chinese Union Version (Simplified and Traditional)

The CUV Simplified (`cus`) and CUV Traditional (`cut`) readings are fetched from GetBible. Its edition listings identify [CUV Simplified](https://getbible.life/cus/) and [CUV Traditional](https://getbible.life/cut/) as public domain and identify FHL as the OSIS source. Preserve this provenance with any locally bundled copy.

### Septuagint (LXX)

The configured LXX source is GetBible's OT LXX Accented edition, attributed to CCAT / University of Pennsylvania. Its [source listing](https://getbible.life/lxx/) states: "Copyrighted; Free non-commercial distribution." Scripture Desk therefore fetches this edition live and does not cache or bundle it. A commercial distribution requires a separate rights review or a replacement source with suitable terms.

## Morphology and Lexicons

### Open Scriptures Hebrew Bible morphology

Hebrew morphology is fetched from the [Open Scriptures Hebrew Bible](https://github.com/openscriptures/morphhb). The work is [CC BY 4.0](https://github.com/openscriptures/morphhb/blob/master/LICENSE.md). Required attribution: "Original work of the Open Scriptures Hebrew Bible available at https://github.com/openscriptures/morphhb".

### MorphGNT SBLGNT morphology

Greek morphology and lemmatization are fetched from [MorphGNT: SBLGNT Edition](https://github.com/morphgnt/sblgnt). The project specifies CC BY-SA for its parsing and lemmatization data. Citation: Tauber, J. K., ed. (2017), *MorphGNT: SBLGNT Edition*, version 6.12 or later.

### Strong's Dictionaries

Lexical glosses are fetched from the [Open Scriptures Strong's Dictionaries](https://github.com/openscriptures/strongs). This app preserves the upstream source credit and does not claim a separate licence for those dynamically fetched entries. Review the upstream repository and any downstream source notices before bundling those dictionaries or creating a derivative dataset.
